03 — Questions

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you run your first simulation.

Is SimCricketX really free?
Yes — completely free, with no credit card required. Create an account with your email and start building squads and simulating matches right away.
Which match formats are supported?
Both T20 and List A (50-over). Each team keeps separate, format-specific squads, so your 50-over specialists are never forced into a T20 line-up.
How does the simulation actually work?
A probabilistic engine resolves every delivery using batting and bowling matchups, the pitch profile you choose, and over-phase dynamics — producing authentic, varied outcomes rather than fixed or purely random results.
Do I need to install anything?
No installation and no config files. SimCricketX runs entirely in your browser — sign up and you're simulating within a couple of minutes.
Can I run full tournaments?
Yes. Set up round-robin or knockout tournaments with automatic standings, point tables, and bracket progression across both formats.
Is my data exportable?
Absolutely — your full match history, scorecards, and player statistics can be downloaded as structured data whenever you need them.
