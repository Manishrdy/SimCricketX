- Fix: moved archiver `_save_to_database()` call in `_handle_tournament_match_completion` to a non-fatal Step 13 after the atomic commit, so a scorecard save failure can never roll back the fixture link or standings
- Fix: wrapped `_check_tournament_completion` and `check_and_progress_tournament` in independent try/except blocks in `update_standings()` â€” a playoff fixture creation error no longer rolls back the standings already applied for the current match
- Added `migrations/repair_tournament_standings.py` â€” one-time data repair script with dry-run and --apply modes; identifies orphaned matches, links them to their fixtures, re-applies standings, and optionally deletes duplicate-play junk rows
